Instant impact for your outdoors

Garden, balcony, patio - you can transform any outside space with minimal effort.
The easiest way to make an interesting display is with a mixture of plant sizes and leaf shapes. Taller plants like Laura and Aggy add a bit of height; they’re also resilient and can survive most conditions. Flowering plants like Lottie and Gia add a welcome burst of colour, while herbs like mint and oregano do double duty - admire them outside then add them to dinner!
Pots will take things to the next level. Keep things clean and elegant with matching pots, or mix styles for something more fun. Whether your plants are destined for a decorative pot or a garden bed, don’t forget your compost and secateurs.
